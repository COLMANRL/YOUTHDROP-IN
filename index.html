Menu

Youth drop-in Initiative is a community- based intervention that targets middle school students and youths at large. Key components include promoting positive norms, planning a future free of risky behaviors and positive interactions with parents and trusted adults.

Join Us Now!

OUR VISION

    To have well behaved, energetic, development conscious and focused community

OUR MISSION

  • To have a well-behaved and healthy society
  • Bringing back youth to good morals
  • To change youth on drugs to better people in society
  • Conserve our ecosystem for posterity

OUR GOALS

    To have well behaved, development concious society

THEME

    Bringing back youths to the good morals
    Okoa kijana kataa madawa ya kulevya

OBJECTIVES

  • To have a generation free from hard drugs usage.
  • To have a well-educated society on their human rights.
  • To create an avenue and resource mobilization and solicit funds through proposals for the organization.
  • To act as a link between the older generation and the young generation.
  • To create awareness on myths and misconceptions about drugs and substance abuse.
  • To link youth with local enterprises for employment.
  • To participate in income-generating activities to uplift one another through table banking.
